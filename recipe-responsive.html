Three favourite Food

Chapati is also known as Indian bread. Made with wheat grain flour. It is a good source of fiber which can be serve with any curry or can be enjoyed by applying some butter on it.

Rice is part of every daily meal in some countires around the world. there are thousands of varity of rice grain available, long aromatic grain varity taste good and healthy for kids.

varan is Indian lentil soup which can be serve with rice or chapati with a spoon full of ghee. It can be made with yellow lentil which are good source of protine and easy to digest.
